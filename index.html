How to Play

1️⃣ Tap “Harvest Letter” to plant a random letter in your farm.

2️⃣ Click two or more matching letters and press “Merge” to combine them and earn coins.

3️⃣ Type real words using your farm letters to earn even more coins! The longer the word, the more coins you get.

💡 Rare letters (Q, Z, J, X) give bonus coins!
