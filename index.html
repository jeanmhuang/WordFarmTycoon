How to Play

🌱 Harvest Letters: Tap the button to plant random letters in your farm!

💥 Merge: Click two or more matching letters and merge them for bonus coins!

✍️ Make Words: Use your letters to create real words — the longer the word, the more coins!

🔥 Combo Bonus: Using rare letters (Q, Z, J, X) gives double rewards!
